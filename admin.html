Role Permissions Matrix

Super Admin:

Full read/write/delete capabilities across all CMS features, user settings, database management, and backups.

Admin:

Can update all site content and review submissions. Restricted from modifying system settings, other user roles, or backups.

Editor:

Can modify static text sections (Hero, About Us), categories, gallery media, and announcements. Cannot view inbox messages, memberships, or events.

Event Manager:

Has full CRUD control over events, upcoming cards, categories, and calendar listings. Cannot edit team members or view messages.

Gallery Manager:

Can upload, remove, filter, and curate images/videos in the gallery grid. Restrained from editing text or schedules.
